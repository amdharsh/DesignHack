FAQ

Questions
answered

Who can participate?
Open to all — students, professionals, hobbyists, and anyone with a passion for design and creativity. No prior hackathon experience needed.
What are the team requirements?
Participate individually or as a team of up to 3 members. Teams can be formed before or during the event. Cross-disciplinary teams are encouraged!
How do I register?
Click "Register Now" anywhere on this page to access our Google Form. Registration details and deadlines will be announced soon. Early registration is recommended as spots are limited.
What are the submission requirements?
Submission formats and deadlines will be shared at the opening session. Your final work should showcase your design concept clearly, creatively, and with strong storytelling.
Will there be guidance or mentoring?
Yes! There's a dedicated check-in and guidance session mid-hackathon where judges and mentors will offer feedback and direction on your work in progress.
How will winners be selected?
Winners are chosen by our judge panel based on creativity, visual appeal, concept strength, storytelling quality, and presentation. Full criteria will be shared at kickoff.
